About Us

FitFoodie is an indian food ordering and delievery plateform. Along with food delievery we also help you check your calorie count of the food time you are ordering.

FitFoodie helps you find and order food from wherever you are. How it works:

You type in an address, we gave you categories underlining with their calorie amount, we tell you the restaurant that deliever to that locale.

Want to be more specific?

Search According to cuisine, restaurant name, menu item or calorie count.
We will filter your results accordingly. You can place your order online or by phone, free of charge.

Oh! and we also give you access to reviews, coupons, special deals, and 24/7 customer care team and Nutritionist team that specify each order and make sure you get exactly what you want.
